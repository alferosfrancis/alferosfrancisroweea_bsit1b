About Us:

Pares or beef pares, is a term for a serving of Filipino braised beef stew with garlic fried rice, and a bowl of clear soup. It is a popular food particularly associated with specialty roadside diner-style establishments known as Pares Houses (Filipino: paresan). In recent years, it had also become a common dish served in small eateries called karinderyas or carinderias that serve economical meals for locals and we sell it with our best seller Pares Overload and other filipino food product! come and enjoy our Pares Near Saint PU Iba, Zambales
